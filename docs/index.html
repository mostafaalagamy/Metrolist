Music Experience
Redefined.

Metrolist is a lightweight, open-source YouTube Music client for Android. Enjoy background playback, ad-free listening, and Material Design 3 aesthetics without the bloat.

Download APK Learn More

Why Metrolist?

Ad-Free Experience

Listen to songs and videos without interruptions. Pure music, zero ads.

Background Play

Keep the music playing even when your screen is off or while using other apps.

Dynamic Themes

Light, Dark, Black, and Dynamic colors based on your album art or system wallpaper.

Offline Mode

Download and cache songs for offline playback to save data on the go.

Discord RPC

Show what you are listening to on your Discord profile automatically (powered by Kizzy).

Audio Tools

Includes Silence Skipper, Audio Normalization, and Pitch/Tempo adjustments.

Sleep Timer

Fall asleep to your favorite tunes. The app stops playing automatically.

Privacy Focused

No trackers. Open Source (GPL-3.0). You are in control of your data.
