Music Experience
Redefined.

Metrolist is a lightweight, open-source YouTube Music client for Android. Enjoy background playback, ad-free listening, and Material Design aesthetics without the bloat.

Download APK Learn More

Why Metrolist?

Ad-Free

Listen to your favorite tracks without any interruptions. Pure music, zero ads.

Background Play

Keep the music playing even when your screen is off or you are using other apps.

Material Design

A beautiful interface that follows modern Android design guidelines (Material 3).

Offline Mode

Download and cache songs for offline playback to save your data.

Live Lyrics

Sing along with synchronized lyrics support directly in the player.

Open Source

Transparent development. Licensed under GPL-3.0. No hidden trackers.
