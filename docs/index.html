Music Experience
Redefined.

Metrolist is a lightweight, open-source YouTube Music client. Experience ad-free listening, background playback, and Material Design 3 aesthetics.

Download APK Learn More

Why Metrolist?

Ad-Free

Enjoy your music without interruptions. No video ads, no audio ads.

Background Play

Keep listening while using other apps or when your screen is locked.

Adaptive Themes

Dynamic colors based on album art (Material You) or pure black mode.

Offline Mode

Download songs and playlists to cache them for offline listening.

Discord RPC

Show your friends what you're listening to automatically via Discord.

Audio Tools

Pitch control, tempo adjustment, silence skipper, and normalization.
